Situated just outside of Caerphilly is the Welsh Answer to Muscle Beach, we pride ourselves on our culture and being known as being more than a gym.


The idea of the gym came from the COVID-19 pandemic where we found that previous office based workers, were missing out on the culture of the workplace where they could engage with peers. We thought why not combine culture with fitness, promoting all aspects of health physical and mental.

The Services we provide:

  • Group Classes
  • Personal Trainers
  • Rehabilitation & Injury Recovery
  • Nutritional Advise
  • Club Running
  • Inhouse competitions
  • Personal Trainers
